Tracking the Fluctuations: Daily Gold Prices from 2018 to 2023.

This dataset contains daily gold prices from April 2018 to March 2023.
Gold is a precious metal that is widely used as a store of value, a hedge against inflation, 
and a safe haven asset in times of economic uncertainty. The dataset includes the daily opening, 
closing, highest, and lowest prices, as well as the volume of gold traded on each day. 
This data could be useful for investors, analysts, and researchers interested in studying 
the fluctuations of gold prices over time and their relation to economic and geopolitical events.
